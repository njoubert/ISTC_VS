The Visual Computing Center was launched on Jan 26th 2011. It is centered at Stanford and brings together thought leaders from Stanford, University of Washington, UC Berkeley, Cornell, Princeton, Harvard, UC Davis, UC Irvine and Intel to collaboratively advance the state-of-the-art in visual computing.
